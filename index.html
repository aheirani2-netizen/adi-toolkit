How to save PDF that looks exactly like this page

iPhone (Safari):

  1. Scroll to top of the page
  2. Tap the Share icon (square with arrow pointing up)
  3. Scroll down and tap Print
  4. Pinch outwards with two fingers on the preview to see all pages
  5. Tap the Share icon again in the top-right → choose Save to Files or Mail

Android (Chrome):

  1. Tap the three dots ⋮ in the top-right corner
  2. Tap Share → Print
  3. Select Save as PDF
  4. Tap the PDF icon or Save button
  5. Choose a folder and save the file

After saving, you can attach the PDF to an email from your Files/Downloads folder.
